Retelling Dubliners

A Walking Tour through Dublin ~ 2 hours

Walk in the footsteps of James Joyce’s Dubliners while learning Dublin’s iconic history. Starting at the James Joyce Centre and ending at Kennedy’s Pub, visit the famous locations mentioned in the book at your own pace.

Tour Stops

  1. 1. James Joyce Center

    The James Joyce Center is a restored Georgian townhouse near the childhood home of James Joyce. Full of exhibitions, readings, lectures, and promoting Joyce’s other works. It’s a great place to get your bearings and expand your knowledge on the Irish famed author.
    Open from 10:30 AM to 4:30 PM Tuesday through Saturday. Tickets are €7 for adults, €5 for seniors and students (with a valid student ID) and free for children 12 and under.
